Fiat from the buyer lands **outside** NoOnes (Skrill, Zelle, Nigerian mobile money like Opay/Paystack, or Mercado Pago).  
You then have to **buy BTC** somewhere using that external fiat, and finally **send the BTC via Lightning** to your NoOnes Lightning deposit address to refill your trading stock.

You want **one Lightning wallet/app** that:
- Lets you buy BTC **directly inside the app** using Skrill / Zelle / Nigerian mobile money / Mercado Pago, **and**
- Then sends that BTC over Lightning to NoOnes.

### What actually exists in 2026 (after checking current services)

**No single Lightning wallet does all of that perfectly in one app.**  
Lightning wallets (Phoenix, Blink, Wallet of Satoshi, Muun, etc.) are mostly non-custodial and **do not have built-in fiat on-ramps** for Skrill, Zelle, Opay, or Mercado Pago. Those payment methods are too regulated for pure Lightning wallets to integrate directly (KYC/AML reasons).

Here’s the **closest real-world systems** that get you as close as possible (minimal steps, high velocity):

**1. Best for Skrill**  
- Use the **Skrill app itself** (it has a built-in “Buy Crypto” section in many countries).  
- Buy BTC directly inside Skrill with your Skrill balance.  
- Withdraw the BTC to a Lightning wallet (Phoenix or Blink) → then send Lightning to your NoOnes deposit address.  
- Speed: Buy in Skrill is instant; Lightning send to NoOnes is 2–10 seconds.  
- This is the closest to “one app for buy + Lightning send”.

**2. Best for Nigerian mobile money (Opay, Paystack, etc.)**  
- **iPayBTC** app (Africa-focused): Buy BTC directly with local mobile money/bank transfer inside the app.  
- Withdraw instantly to any Lightning wallet (Phoenix, Blink, Wallet of Satoshi).  
- From there send Lightning to NoOnes deposit.  
- Speed: Buy + withdraw in under 2–5 minutes total. Many Nigerian merchants use this exact flow.

**3. Best for Mercado Pago (Argentina)**  
- No direct Lightning wallet has Mercado Pago built-in.  
- Closest: Use **MT Pelerin** or **Lightning Pay** services (you buy sats directly to any Lightning wallet address via Mercado Pago).  
- Or go through a fast ramp like **Ramp Network** / **MoonPay** that accepts Mercado Pago in some setups and sends straight to Lightning.  
- Then from your Lightning wallet (Phoenix/Blink) send to NoOnes.  
- Speed: 3–8 minutes end-to-end.

**4. Best for Zelle (US)**  
- Zelle is the hardest (US banks flag crypto heavily).  
- Use a ramp like **Ramp Network** or **MoonPay** that accepts bank transfers (Zelle-linked), buy BTC, send to Lightning wallet, then to NoOnes.  
- No clean “inside one Lightning wallet” option.

### Recommended Lightning wallets for the final send to NoOnes
(These are the ones you actually use to send the bought BTC to NoOnes Lightning deposit):

- **Phoenix** (best overall – self-custodial, reliable liquidity, 5–10 sec sends)  
- **Blink** (fastest for small amounts, sub-2 sec in many cases)  
- **Wallet of Satoshi** (easiest UI, instant)  
- **Zeus** or **Breez** (if you want more control at higher volume)

### The practical loop you can run today
1. Buyer pays you via Skrill / Mercado Pago / Opay → fiat in external service.  
2. Buy BTC in the best ramp for that method (Skrill app / iPayBTC / MT Pelerin / Ramp).  
3. Send the BTC via Lightning from Phoenix or Blink to your NoOnes Lightning deposit address (2–10 seconds).  
4. Stock refilled in NoOnes → post next sell ad.

This is the tightest system that exists right now. It keeps the Lightning part inside a real Lightning wallet (as you wanted) and minimizes external hops.
